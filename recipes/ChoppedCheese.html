Chopped CHeese Recipe

This Recipe uses many flavourful ingredients for an easy, yet amazing dish

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Ground beef
  • Peppers, onions
  • Bread Roll

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Chop the lettuce, tomatoes, peppers, and onions
  3. Toast the bread roll
  4. Put it all together
